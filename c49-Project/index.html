Interesting Facts about Camel

  • Camels are mammals.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles
  • They are cold blooded animals.
  • They have the ability to camoflauge into their surroundings.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are aves and warm blooded animals.
  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.

Interesting Facts about Bears

  • Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals.
  • Bears grieve deeply for others.
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Theydo not hibernate.
  • Giant pandas are shy animals that prefer to live alone.
  • Giant pandas are born blind.

Interesting Facts about Cicads

  • Most have red-orange eyes.
  • They improve lawns by digging tunnels that allow air into the soil.
  • They have a short adult life.
